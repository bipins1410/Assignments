Kalinga War 261 B.C. Ashoka and Raja Anant Padmanabhan Ashoka It was the bloodiest war of human history and lakhs were killed from either sides. Ashoka filled with remorse and took pledge of non violence and adopted Buddhism.
First Battle of Tarain 1191 A.D. Prithviraj Chauhan and Muhammad Ghori Prithviraj defeated Ghori This battle fought betweenThis is called the first war of Hindu Rajput king and Muslim invader. This war took place near Tarain, Thanesar distt of Haryana.
First Battle of Panipat 1526 A.D. Babur and Ibrahim Lodi Babar defeats Ibrahim Lodhi Canons, Gunpowder and artilery was used by Babur which was the first time ever use of these in Indian subcontinent. Ibrahim Lodi was killed in the field and this war paved the foundation of strong Mughal Empire in India.
Battle of Ghaghara 1529 A.D. Babur and Sultan Mahmud Lodi Babar defeats the Afghans This was the third major battle of Babur after which control of Mughal Empire over India was secured and fully established. Mahmud Lodi who was the brother of Ibrahim Lodi declared himself the sultan of Delhi with the support of Nusrat shah of Bengal, Jalaluddin Lohani, Sher Shah Suri , Rajputs and Afghans. Babur emerged victorious and consolidated his control.
Battle of Haldighati 1576 A.D. Maharana Pratap & Mughal Emperor Akbar, who was led by Man Singh Undecisive battle between Raja Man Singh of Mughal Army and Rana Pratap of Mewar Rana Pratap fought gallantly and took refuge in a remote fortress.This battle is the shortest battle of Indian history and lasted only 4 hours.
